Common Questions

F.A.Q

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends. While we accept walk-ins if space is available, booking online guarantees your spot.

Is there a height requirement?

Yes. For our Junior karts, minimum height is 4'3". For our Adult karts, minimum height is 4'10". All drivers must also fit safely in kart.

Do you provide safety gear?

Absolutely. We provide a full-face helmet, a balaclava (head sock), and a racing suit at no extra cost with your rental.

Can I bring my own helmet?

Yes, provided it is a full-face helmet with a clear visor and meets DOT safety standards. Our marshals reserve right to inspect it before use.
