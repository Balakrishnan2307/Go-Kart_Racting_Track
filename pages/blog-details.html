Track Surface Update

New Track Surface Installation Completed

Alex Rossi Oct 24, 2023 3 Comments

We are thrilled to announce that the track resurfacing project, which began three weeks ago, has been successfully completed. Our goal was simple: create one of the fastest, safest, and most durable surfaces for our drivers.

The Technology Behind the Asphalt

We utilized a specialized polymer-modified asphalt typically reserved for Formula 1 circuits. This provides significantly higher grip levels, especially in the wet, reducing braking distances and increasing cornering speeds.

"The grip level is insane compared to last season. I dropped two seconds off my personal best on the first day of testing. It feels like a brand new track!"
- Mark Johnson, League Champion

What This Means for You

For our casual drivers, this means a more predictable drive and less tire wear. For our racers, get ready to smash those lap records! We have already re-calibrated our timing systems to match the new surface.

Alex Rossi

Founder & Track Director. Former F1 mechanic with a passion for grassroots racing.
