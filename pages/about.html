Our Story

From a dusty blueprint to the region's premier racing circuit.

Founded in 2010

Built by Racers, For Racers

TurboTrack began with a simple idea: create a safe haven for speed lovers. Founded by former Formula 1 mechanic Alex "Gears" Rossi, our track was carved out of an old industrial warehouse complex.

Over the last decade, we have expanded from a single indoor loop to a massive outdoor complex featuring three distinct track configurations. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of asphalt and kart maintenance.

Uncompromising Safety

We take your safety seriously.

Every driver receives a comprehensive briefing before hitting the tarmac. We provide full-face helmets, racing suits, and gloves for all sessions. Our karts are equipped with wrap-around bumper systems and remote shutdown capabilities controlled by track marshals.
