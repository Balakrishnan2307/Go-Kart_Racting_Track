Our History

From Asphalt to Electric Dreams

Founded in 2023, Velocity Raceway was born from a simple idea: racing should be accessible, safe, and exhilarating for everyone. Our founders, former professional racers and engineers, grew tired of the noise and fumes of traditional gas tracks.

We invested in state-of-the-art electric karts that offer instant torque, zero emissions, and consistent performance for every driver. We carved out our 1.2km circuit to challenge even the most seasoned pros, while keeping it approachable for beginners.

Technical Specs

The Circuit Layout

Designed by former F1 track architects, our 1.2km circuit features 18 demanding corners. It is a technical circuit that rewards smooth braking and precise lines.

  • Turns: 18 Total (9 Left, 9 Right)
  • Straight: 300m Main Straight (Top Speed Zone)
  • Width: 8 to 10 Meters (Wide Overtaking Areas)
  • Elevation: Minimal change (Flat circuit for consistency)
  • Surface: High-Grip Asphalt
